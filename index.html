Meet Beau

He is a ten years old, cross-eyed scaredy cat. He runs from doorbells, loud noises and people attempting to pet him. He enjoys playing with plastic, laying on paper and sitting in bags.

Some of Beau's Lovely Features

  • Meow for attention but run away when you try to give it to him
  • Brush up against your legs when you wear black pants
  • Sit on laundry fresh out of the dryer (before you can fold it)
  • Knock over all my plants

Beau's Nicknames

  • Bil-Beau Baggins
  • Bo Shmo
  • Beau Cat Shmo Cat
  • Fat Cat

I love Beau. I wish he was more of a cuddley cat but he's still sweet in his own stand-offish way. This last Christmas I got him a new brush as a present. I spent an entire day making a box in the shape of a paw print to wrap it in. He didn't care.

One day I want to get a new pet to introduce what I consider to be normal pet behavior. Then I could create a new website titled Beau & New Pet. Until then it's just Beau Shmo
